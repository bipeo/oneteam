INSTALLATION & SERVIS

Today MTE is a world-wide group of companies focising in AV integration and content business. Starting from 1975 we strongly experienced at the entertainment & motion picture field. During these time we planned, delivered and installed more than 3500 AV professional systems for cinemas all over the world. Besides providing itegrator services we are experienced in operating our own cinemas in different parts of the world (USA, Russia, Middle East). This gives our customers acess to our unique experience which making their projects to be succeed.

Through its various affiliates, MTE provides: distribution of projection, sound and post production equipment; post-production facilities for digital and analog film production; design consulting for the development of live, performing and motion picture theatres.
